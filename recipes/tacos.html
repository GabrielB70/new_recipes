Tacos Recipe

Description:

Authentic Mexican tacos served on warm corn tortillas and filled with tender,
flavorful meat, fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a squeeze of lime.
Packed with smoky, savory flavors and customizable with salsa and toppings,
tacos are a vibrant and iconic street food loved for their simplicity, freshness, and bold taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa

Steps:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water according to package instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  4. Assemble tacos by filling each tortilla with seasoned beef, lettuce, tomatoes,cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
